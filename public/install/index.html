What the Installer Does

Three steps, fully automated. Here's what happens behind the scenes.

1

Checks & Clones

Verifies Python 3.10+ is installed, then clones the Crew Bus repo to your machine.

2

Installs Dependencies

Sets up a virtual environment, installs Python packages, and installs Ollama for local AI.

3

Launches Your Crew

Starts the dashboard — your crew is live. Guardian walks you through the rest.
