Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Who We Are

crew-bus ("we", "us", "our") is an open-source software project that provides AI agent crew management infrastructure. Our website is crew-bus.dev.

2. What We Collect

2.1 Core Software (Free)

The core crew-bus software runs entirely on your local machine. We do not collect, transmit, or store any data from your local installation. Your messages, agent configurations, decisions, and audit logs stay on your device.

2.2 Website Visitors

When you visit crew-bus.dev, we may collect:

  • Pages viewed and time spent (via privacy-respecting analytics)
  • Referring website
  • Country (derived from IP, IP is not stored)
  • Browser type and screen size

We use privacy-respecting analytics that does not use cookies or track individuals.

2.3 Guard Activation (Paid)

When you purchase the Security Guard module, Stripe collects and processes your payment information. We receive:

  • Email address (for sending your activation key)
  • Payment confirmation (amount, date)
  • Stripe customer ID

We never see or store your credit card number, expiration date, or CVV. All payment processing is handled by Stripe.

2.4 Installer Marketplace

If you sign up as an installer, we collect:

  • Name and contact information (email)
  • Service area (postal code / ZIP)
  • Specialties and professional information
  • KYC verification: We store only a SHA-256 hash of your government ID. The raw image is never stored on our servers.
  • Reviews and ratings from clients

2.5 Job Board

If you post or claim a job, we collect the information you provide: job description, location (postal code), budget range, and contact details.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver the Guard activation key after purchase
  • To display your installer profile in the marketplace (if you sign up)
  • To match jobs with installers by location
  • To send transactional emails (purchase confirmations, job notifications)
  • To improve the website and user experience

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as needed for payment processing (Stripe).

4. Data Storage and Security

Our servers are hosted on Hetzner in the EU (Germany). Data is stored in SQLite databases with encrypted backups. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all connections
  • Firewall rules limiting open ports
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

5. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — Payment processing. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Hetzner — Server hosting in the EU.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

For any privacy-related requests, contact us at privacy@crew-bus.dev.

7. Cookies

The crew-bus.dev website does not use tracking cookies. If you log in to the installer marketplace, we use a session cookie strictly necessary for maintaining your login state. No third-party cookies are set.

8. Data Retention

  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years (tax/legal requirements)
  • Installer profiles: Retained while your account is active, deleted upon request
  • Job postings: Retained for 1 year after completion, then anonymized
  • Analytics data: Aggregated, no individual tracking, retained for 2 years

9. Children

crew-bus is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced on the website. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact
