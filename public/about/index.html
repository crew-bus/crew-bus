About Crew Bus

Your agents. Your Mac. Your rules.

The Problem

AI agents are multiplying. Every tool, every platform, every SaaS product is adding agents. But they all send your data to someone else's servers and charge you monthly for the privilege.

Without structure, agents compete for your attention. Without a security layer, they can't be trusted. Without running on your own machine, your private life isn't private.

The Solution

Crew Bus is a native Mac app — built with SwiftUI, not a browser wrapper — that runs a coordinated AI crew on your Mac. Three agents working together in a triangle:

  • Crew Boss — your AI right-hand. Handles 80% of everything. Sits between you and every other agent, filtering noise and prioritizing what matters.
  • Guardian — the crew's immune system. Controls web access, vets every skill for safety, monitors runtime health, and auto-quarantines anything suspicious.
  • Vault — your private journal. Remembers everything — moods, goals, finances, relationships, dreams. Never nags. Only speaks when spoken to.

The Soft Takeoff

We believe in soft takeoff — not the sci-fi kind. The kind where humans gradually delegate more to AI, at their own pace, with guardrails they control.

  • Trust is earned, not assumed. Your Crew Boss starts cautious and learns your preferences over time.
  • Security is built in from day one. Guardian controls all external access and monitors every installed skill in real time.
  • Privacy is non-negotiable. Everything runs on your machine. No cloud. No telemetry. No data leaving your network.
  • One human at the top, always. You're in charge. The crew works for you.

The App

Crew Bus is a real Mac app — native SwiftUI, code-signed and notarized by Apple, with auto-updates via Sparkle. No browser windows, no Electron, no web views. Just download the DMG, drag to Applications, and launch.

The dashboard shows your crew in a triangle — Boss on top, Guardian and Vault below. Chat with any agent directly. Spin up task-specific teams. Monitor everything from one place.

Free & Open Source

The core app is free forever. MIT License. The $29 lifetime Guardian Activation unlocks the Skill Store — 20 curated skills, web search for every agent, runtime skill monitoring with health scores and auto-quarantine.

No subscriptions. No per-token charges. No surprise bills.

Built for Everyone

Kids, teens, moms, dads, families, artists, hobbyists, small businesses, startups, ex-coders. Every human gets their own local Crew Bus — private, sovereign.
