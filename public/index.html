Up and Running in 5 Minutes

No tech skills needed. Three steps. macOS 14.0 or later.

1

Download

Download the DMG, drag Crew Bus to Applications. No sign-ups, no cloud accounts, no credit card.

2

Meet Your Crew

Launch the app. Guardian walks you through setup. Crew Boss asks a few questions to calibrate. Vault opens its pages. Three agents, ready to go.

3

Talk to Crew Boss

Your dashboard lights up with a triangle — Boss on top, Guardian and Vault below. Just start talking. Your crew takes it from there.
