What crew-bus does

Everything you need to run a crew of AI agents — and stay in control.

★

Crew Boss

AI chief of staff that routes messages, filters noise, and learns your preferences. Trust slider from 1 (observer) to 10 (full autonomy).

◑

Chain of Command

Enforced hierarchy: You → Crew Boss → Core Agents → Departments. No agent bypasses the chain unless you allow it.

♥

Burnout Awareness

Built-in burnout score (1-10). When you're running hot, non-urgent messages get queued. Your crew protects your energy.

☾

Quiet Hours

Set your off hours. Only critical messages get through. Everything else waits for morning. Timezone-aware.

✉

Multi-Channel Delivery

Telegram, Email, Signal, or console. Each agent gets its own channel. Plug in whatever you use.

⚖

Morning Briefings

Wake up to a compiled brief from your Crew Boss. Key decisions, overnight activity, and what needs your attention today.

🔒

Private Sessions

1-on-1 encrypted conversations with any agent. Crew Boss logs the occurrence but never sees the content.

⚗

Team Mailbox

Any agent can escalate to the team mailbox when the chain of command isn't enough. Code red alerts go straight to you.

How It Works

Three steps. Five minutes. Your crew is running.

1

Install

pip install crew-bus

One command. No cloud accounts. No API keys. Runs on your machine.

2

Configure

crew-bus init

Answer a few questions. Name your crew. Set your quiet hours. Pick your channels.

3

Run

crew-bus dashboard

Open localhost:8080. Your circle dashboard is live. Five agents, one Crew Boss, and you at the top.

The Dashboard

A command center you can see at a glance. Dark theme. Circle layout. Every agent visible.
