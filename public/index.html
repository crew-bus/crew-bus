What Your Crew Does for You

Think of it like a team of friendly helpers — each one good at something different, all working together.

★

Crew Boss

Your helpful right-hand who handles 80% of everything so you don't have to. Rename them anything you like — Buddy, Sis, Captain, whatever feels right.

◑

You're Always in Charge

Every agent answers to you through Crew Boss. Nothing happens without your say-so. Adjust the trust slider anytime — give your crew more freedom or keep a closer eye.

♥

Looks Out for You

Your crew tracks how busy you are. When things get hectic, non-urgent stuff waits. Health Buddy can step in on a Code Red — because your wellbeing comes first.

☾

Quiet Hours

Set your downtime and your crew respects it. Only truly important stuff gets through. Everything else waits until you're ready. Evenings and weekends are yours.

✉

Reaches You Anywhere

Get updates on Telegram, Email, Signal, or right on screen — wherever is easiest for you. Each agent can message you on the channel you prefer.

⚖

Morning Catch-Up

Wake up to a friendly summary from your Crew Boss — what happened overnight, what needs your attention today, and any decisions waiting for you. Like a good friend catching you up.

🔒

Private Conversations

Need to talk to an agent one-on-one? Private sessions are encrypted just for you. Your Crew Boss knows the chat happened but never sees what was said.

⚗

Family & Passion Modes

Turn on Family Mode for shared chores, art jams, and family projects. Or Passion Mode for your creative side — Muse gives you prompts, tracks your streak, and cheers you on.

Up and Running in 5 Minutes

Seriously — three steps and your crew is ready to help. No accounts, no cloud, no fuss.

1

Install

pip install crew-bus

One quick command on your computer. No sign-ups, no cloud accounts. Everything stays on your machine, private and yours.

2

Meet Your Crew

crew-bus init

A friendly wizard asks a few questions — what you need help with, when your quiet hours are, what to call your Crew Boss. That's it.

3

Say Hi

crew-bus dashboard

Your dashboard lights up — Crew Boss in the middle, five agents around the circle, and you at the top. Your crew is ready. Just start talking.
