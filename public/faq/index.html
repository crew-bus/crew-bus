Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is Crew Bus?

Crew Bus is a native Mac app that gives you a personal AI crew — three agents working together on your own hardware. Crew Boss handles 80% of everything, Guardian keeps you safe, and Vault remembers your whole life privately. Free, open source, no cloud.

How is Crew Bus different from other agent frameworks?

Most agent frameworks are developer tools that require hours of setup and send your data through the cloud. Crew Bus is a real Mac app you download and launch in minutes. It comes with a built-in security agent (Guardian), a private journal agent (Vault), and a coordinator (Crew Boss) — all running locally. No other framework ships with an immune system.

What agents come built-in?

Three core agents: Crew Boss (your AI right-hand), Guardian (security and web access control), and Vault (private journal and life-data). You can also spin up task-specific teams with additional agents.

Do I need cloud accounts or API keys?

No. Crew Bus runs entirely on your machine. The only external calls are to your chosen LLM provider (for AI responses) and optionally to Stripe (for Guardian Activation payment). Your data never leaves your machine.

The App

What kind of app is this?

A native SwiftUI Mac app — not a web page in a wrapper. No browser windows, no Electron, no web views. Code-signed and notarized by Apple. Auto-updates via Sparkle.

What are the system requirements?

macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later. Apple Silicon or Intel Mac. 4 GB+ RAM (8 GB recommended). About 500 MB disk space.

Is there a Windows or Linux version?

Not yet. Crew Bus is macOS-only right now. Windows and Linux are on the roadmap.

How do I install it?

Download the DMG from crew-bus.dev/install, drag Crew Bus to Applications, and launch. Three steps, five minutes.

Security & Privacy

What does Guardian do?

Guardian is your crew's immune system. It controls all web access, vets every skill before installation, monitors installed skills in real time with health scores (0-100), auto-quarantines anything that degrades, and provides a full audit trail. It also scans for prompt injection, data exfiltration, and jailbreak attempts.

Are private sessions really private?

Yes. When you talk to Vault, the content stays between you and Vault. No other agent sees it. Everything is stored locally in SQLite on your machine.

Where is my data stored?

Locally. SQLite database on your Mac. No telemetry. No cloud sync. No data leaves your machine unless you explicitly tell it to (e.g., web search via Guardian).

Pricing

Is the core really free forever?

Yes. MIT License. The full native Mac app with all three agents (Crew Boss, Guardian, Vault), the dashboard, chat, auto-updates, and 6 LLM providers — free forever. No trial periods, no feature gates, no usage limits.

What does the $29 Guardian Activation unlock?

Web search for every agent (powered by DuckDuckGo, no API key needed). The Skill Store with 20 curated skills across 10 categories. Runtime skill monitoring with health scores, error tracking, and auto-quarantine. One payment, lifetime key, never expires.

Are there subscriptions?

No. $29 once, forever. No recurring charges. No per-token fees. No surprise bills.

Installer Marketplace

What is the installer marketplace?

It connects people who want Crew Bus set up with verified professionals who do the setup — remotely or in person. Installers are verified, rated by the community, and searchable by location.

Can I use my own LLM/AI provider?

Yes. Crew Bus supports multiple LLM providers including Kimi, Claude, OpenAI, Groq, Gemini, and Ollama (fully local). You can pick a different model for each agent.
