FAQ

Is the core really free forever?

Yes. MIT License. The full native Mac app with all three agents (Crew Boss, Guardian, Vault), INTEGRITY.md and CREW_CHARTER.md protections, private sessions, 6 LLM providers, and the full dashboard — all free forever. No trial periods, no feature gates, no usage limits.

What does the $29 Guardian Activation unlock?

Guardian is already free and always on — it's your setup guide and protector from day one. The $29 activation unlocks the Skill Store (downloadable skills that make any agent smarter) plus the full security vetting pipeline: prompt injection scanning, data exfiltration detection, jailbreak blocking, hash-based registry, and three-tier approval. One-time payment, lifetime key, never expires.

What are INTEGRITY.md and CREW_CHARTER.md?

INTEGRITY.md is core rules in every agent's brain — no agent can gaslight you, rewrite history, or be condescending. But they CAN give you honest perspective when you need it — that's what a good right-hand does. CREW_CHARTER.md is shared values every agent lives by — honesty, care, competence, and teamwork. These protections are free and always active. No other AI system has anything like this.

What AI models are supported?

Six providers: Kimi K2.5 (free tier available), Claude, OpenAI, Groq, Gemini, and Ollama (fully local, no API key needed). You can pick a different model for each agent. Crew Boss typically runs on the best model available because it handles 80% of everything.

Is my data sent to the cloud?

No. Crew Bus runs 100% locally on your Mac. SQLite database on your machine. No telemetry, no tracking, no analytics, no ads. The only network calls are to your chosen LLM provider (for AI responses) and Stripe (for payments). Your data never leaves your machine.

Can a kid use this?

Yes. Crew Bus is designed so a 10-year-old can figure it out. Guardian walks you through setup. The dashboard is visual and friendly. There's a PIN lock so kids can't accidentally change things. And since everything runs locally with no data collection, it's the safest AI experience available for young people.
