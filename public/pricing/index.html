FAQ

Is the core really free forever?

Yes. MIT License. Your entire inner circle (Crew Boss, Guardian, Wellness, Strategy, Communications, Financial, Knowledge, Legal), all their unique skills, INTEGRITY.md protection, CREW_CHARTER.md enforcement, Inner Circle Protocol, private sessions, burnout awareness, 6 LLM providers, 3 free team templates, and the full dashboard — all free forever. No trial periods, no feature gates, no usage limits.

What does the $29 Guardian activation unlock?

Guardian is already free and always on — it's your setup guide and basic protector from day one. The $29 activation unlocks the Skill Store: downloadable skills that make any agent smarter, plus the full security vetting pipeline (prompt injection scanning, data exfiltration detection, jailbreak blocking, hash-based registry, three-tier approval). One-time payment, lifetime key, never expires.

What are INTEGRITY.md and CREW_CHARTER.md?

INTEGRITY.md is a set of sacred rules injected into every agent's brain — no agent can ever gaslight you, dismiss your feelings, sugarcoat bad news, or say "you're overreacting." CREW_CHARTER.md is a constitution for all worker agents — they must be honest, competent, caring, and disciplined. Two violations = fired. These protections are free and always active. No other AI system has anything like this.

Does one team license cover unlimited teams?

No. Each license covers one team. If you need 3 departments, that's 3 Department licenses. This keeps pricing fair — you only pay for what you use.

What AI models are supported?

Six providers: Kimi K2.5 (free tier available), Claude, OpenAI, Groq, Gemini, and Ollama (fully local, no API key needed). You can pick a different model for each agent. Crew Boss typically runs on the best model available because it's worth the investment — it handles 80% of everything.

Is my data sent to the cloud?

No. Crew Bus runs 100% locally on your hardware. SQLite database on your machine. No telemetry, no tracking, no analytics, no ads. The only network calls are to your chosen LLM provider (for AI responses) and Stripe (for payments). Your data never leaves your machine.

Can a kid use this?

Yes. Crew Bus is designed so a 10-year-old can figure it out. Guardian walks you through setup. The dashboard is visual and mobile-first. There's a PIN lock so kids can't accidentally delete agents. And since everything runs locally with no data collection, it's the safest AI experience available for young people.
