FAQ

Is the core really free forever?

Yes. MIT License. The full message bus, all core agents, dashboard, CLI, and every feature listed under Core is free forever. No trial periods, no feature gates, no usage limits.

What does the $20 Guard activation get me?

The Security Guard module adds anomaly detection, threat monitoring across 6 domains, automatic escalation, and audit hardening. It's a one-time payment — no subscription. The activation key works forever.

Why is the first installer permit free?

We want good installers to try the marketplace without risk. Your first permit is free. After that, each permit costs $25 and includes 6 months of Guardian key messaging.

Do I need to pay to use the installer marketplace as a client?

No. Posting jobs and browsing installers is free for clients. You only pay the installer directly for their services.

Is my data sent to the cloud?

No. crew-bus runs locally. SQLite database on your machine. No telemetry. No cloud accounts. The only network calls are when you use Stripe for payment or external delivery channels (Telegram, email).

⚡ Payments secured by Stripe. We never see your card details.
